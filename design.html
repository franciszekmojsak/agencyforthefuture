design process

A critical journey from idea to the implementation of a successful & meaningful product

  1. Understanding clients needs and goals
  2. Immersion in users world and product environment
  3. Ideation with various scenarios and product storytelling
  4. Iterations of building and testing different directions
  5. Analysis of potential product life-cycle and circular solutions
  6. Definition of products functionalities and construction
  7. Product CMF (color, material, finishing) options considering sustainable materials
  8. Cooperation with manufacturers in building high fidelity prototypes
  9. Design of product packaging and all the manuals documentation
  10. Supervision over production process

circular economy – core of our design process

Circular economy — core of our design process

Our agency is dedicated to fostering a sustainable world through circular design practices. Our goal is to actively contribute to the transition towards a circular economy, guiding businesses from linear to circular models through purposeful design interventions.
