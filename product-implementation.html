implementation portfolio

Products brought to market

Vovo Glasses

Design: Ewa Hiller | Materials: borosilicate, coloured glass

Vovo Glasses are an object of art, born from fascination with two basic geometric forms. Coloured glass elements create various hues and an illusion of changing shapes.

Piano Table

Design: Ewa Hiller | Materials: handcrafted solid wood, varnished or painted

Designed around the ritual of extending the table. Made of solid wood, the piece has an austere structure and clean lines. Its power of expression stems from simplicity.

Mood Mirror

Design: Dorothee Mainka | Materials: coloured mirror and powder coated steel frame

Collection of three wall mirrors of different proportions and colours. The metal shelf embraces the mirror face and wanders up each site, providing shelf space for small everyday objects.

Bowler Mug

Design: Ola Mirecka | Materials: moulded and fired, unglazed porcelain

Designed for relaxed celebration of hot beverages. Made of specially developed porcelain that doesn't require glazing. The matt surface makes it unique and will age beautifully.

Cork Stool

Design: Tomasz Rygalik | Materials: turned cork, expanded or natural

Shape inspired by the material it is made of. Eco-friendly, highly endurable and very light, cork still seems underexploited in furniture design.

Dee Table

Design: Fabio Hendry & Martijn Rigters | Materials: anodized aluminum

Simple form of aluminium d-shaped side table resonates its modern look. Special advantage of anodised aluminium includes corrosion and scratch resistance.
